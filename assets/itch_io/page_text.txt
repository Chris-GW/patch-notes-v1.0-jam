<blockquote>A tiny <strong>hack & slash</strong> &mdash; defend your village against waves of plundering ducks
   <br>Patch Notes Jam v1.0 &mdash; Theme: "The Error Is the Feature"
</blockquote>
<h4>About</h4>
<p>You play as <strong>Murphy, the mad swordsman</strong> &mdash; a fighter whose greatest weakness has become his only weapon.&nbsp;Murphy must embrace his cursed fighting style to protect his family and rescue villages from plundering ducks.</p>
<ul>
   <li>His sword swings look normal&hellip; but the damage lands seconds later.</li>
   <li>His ghostly afterimages confuse enemies just as much as they confuse him.</li>
   <li>His dash unleashes bursts of speed, but leaves him struggling to steer.</li>
</ul>
<h4>How to Play</h4>
<ul>
   <li><strong>Explore</strong> your homeland and rescue villages in desperate need.</li>
   <li><strong>Fight</strong> relentless waves of invading ducks and master your unique fighting style.</li>
   <li><strong>Survive</strong> long enough until the evil&nbsp;ducks are gone for good.</li>
</ul>
